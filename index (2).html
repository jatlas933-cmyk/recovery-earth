AI Environmental Technology

We are building intelligent monitoring systems that combine sensors, computer vision, and automation to improve environmental management. These systems allow real-time understanding of ecosystems and help governments and organizations respond faster to environmental change.

Fish Conservation Systems

Our aquatic technology focuses on solving one of the biggest problems in freshwater ecosystems: protecting native fish while controlling invasive species. We are developing smart fish passage systems that use sensors and AI recognition to guide wildlife safely through water infrastructure while removing harmful invasive species.
