What We Build

AI-Powered Environmental Infrastructure

We develop intelligent monitoring systems that combine computer vision, sensors, and automation to give ecosystems a voice — and give conservationists the tools to respond.

🔬

Computer Vision

Real-time species identification using deep learning models trained on native and invasive aquatic wildlife across Florida's waterways.

📡

Citizen Reporting

Mobile-first progressive web apps that let everyday Floridians report invasive sightings, upload photos, and contribute to ecosystem data.

🏛️

Conservation Infrastructure

Smart fish passage systems that guide native species safely through water infrastructure while autonomously filtering out invasive threats.

Flagship Project

Florida Waterways Guardian

Our AI classifier identifies fish species in real time — distinguishing native wildlife from invasive threats like Bowfin, Mayan cichlids, and oscar fish.

  • 44-class YOLOv8 model in active training
  • Deployable offline via progressive web app
  • English / Spanish bilingual interface
  • Integrated citizen reporting with live data dashboard
  • Built for schools, anglers, and agency partners
